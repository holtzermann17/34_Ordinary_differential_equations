Lyapunov stable


A fixed point x* is Lyapunov stable if trajectories of nearby points x remain close for future time. More formally the fixed point x* is Lyapunov stable, if for any ϵ>0, there is a δ>0 such that for all t≥0 and for all x≠x* it is verified

d⁢(x*,x)<δ⇒d⁢(x*,x⁢(t))<ϵ.

In particular, d⁢(x*,x⁢(0))=0.
