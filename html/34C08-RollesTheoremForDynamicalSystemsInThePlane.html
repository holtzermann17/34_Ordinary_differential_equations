Rolle’s theorem for dynamical systems in the plane


Let ϕ be a smooth planar dynamical system and γ a continuous curve which has a tangent vector at each point (i.e. γ is differentiable). Then there is a contact point between any two points of the intersection of γ and a separating solution of ϕ. [KGA]
