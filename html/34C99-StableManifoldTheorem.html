stable manifold theorem


Let E be an open subset of ℝn containing the origin, let f∈C1⁢(E), and let ϕt be the flow of the nonlinear system x′=f⁢(x).

Suppose that f⁢(x0)=0 and that D⁢f⁢(x0) has k eigenvalues with negative real part and n-k eigenvalues with positive real part. Then there exists a k-dimensional differentiable manifold S tangent to the stable subspace ES of the linear system x′=D⁢f⁢(x)⁢x at x0 such that for all t≥0, ϕt⁢(S)⊂S and for all y∈S,

limt→∞⁡ϕt⁢(y)=x0

and there exists an n-k dimensional differentiable manifold U tangent to the unstable subspace EU of x′=D⁢f⁢(x)⁢x at x0 such that for all t≤0, ϕt⁢(U)⊂U and for all y∈U,

limt→-∞⁡ϕt⁢(y)=x0.
