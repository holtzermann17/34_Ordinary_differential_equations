Landau-Ramanujan Constant


Let N be a natural number. Consider the equation

x2+y2=N

Let L⁢(N) denote the number of naturals z≤N such that the equation x2+y2=z has at least one integer solution (x,y). The Laudau-Ramanujan constant is defined as the limit

limN→∞⁡L⁢(N)⁢log⁡(N)N≈0.76422365358922066299069873125
