symmetry of an ordinary differential equation


Let f:ℝn→ℝn be a smooth function and let

x˙=f⁢(x)

be a system of ordinary differential equations, in addition let γ be an invertible matrix. Then γ is a  of the ordinary differential equation if

f⁢(γ⁢x)=γ⁢f⁢(x).

  • •

    Natural symmetry of the Lorenz equation is a example of a symmetry of a differential equation.
